Zoe's Morning

Zoe wakes up, feeling that today will be a special day. She gets dressed in her favorite yellow dress, has breakfast, and gets ready to go outside.

Zoe in the Park

Zoe walks into the park, admiring the beautiful flowers and listening to the birds singing. She feels happy and decides to play on the grass.

Zoe Meets a Puppy

As Zoe sits on the grass, a little puppy runs over, wagging its tail like a small flag. Zoe smiles and says, "Hi, little puppy, how are you?"

Zoe Becomes Friends with the Puppy

The puppy gently nudges Zoe’s hand with its nose. Zoe strokes its head and says, "You're so friendly. We can play together!"

Zoe and the Puppy Run Together

Zoe holds the puppy's leash, and they start running through the park. They run across the grass and over a small stream, laughing together.

Zoe Teaches the Puppy a New Game

Zoe stops and decides to teach the puppy how to play fetch. She throws the ball far away, and the puppy quickly runs to fetch it, bringing it back to Zoe.

Zoe Discovers a Beautiful Flower

After the game, Zoe notices a beautiful flower growing in the grass. She carefully picks it and says to the puppy, "This flower is so pretty. Let's look at it together."

Zoe Learns to Share

Zoe looks at the flower and thinks, "I shouldn't just enjoy this flower by myself, I can share it with the puppy." She gently places the flower next to the puppy.

Zoe Says Goodbye to the Puppy

As the sun begins to set, Zoe knows it’s time to go home. She gently pats the puppy’s head and says, "Goodbye, thank you for playing with me today!"

Zoe’s Happy Walk Home

Zoe happily walks home, thinking about all the things she learned today and how she made a new friend. She looks forward to seeing the puppy again tomorrow and playing more games together.
